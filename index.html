Our Story

We are a small nonprofit dedicated to bringing safe, clean water to communities around the world. For over a decade we've partnered with local leaders to build wells, install filtration systems, and run education programs so children can go to school instead of fetching water.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
